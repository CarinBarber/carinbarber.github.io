About Me

My name's Carin Barber and this is my fourth semester in the Software Development program. Once I complete this I'll be continuing on with the web development program. I currently do freelance work and have a cat named MJ. She's fantastic. My favorite and most expensive hobby is traveling but there's honestly nothing better. I've been dabbling with German, Russian, Spanish, and Italian for a few years which works well with my love of travel. I've also been teaching myself guitar and piano and enjoy baking and being outdoors, learning and trying new things, reading, knitting, gaming, and listening to podcasts. I would like to move to England in the next five years to work remotely and continue my travels before retiring at a reasonable age to dive headfirst into my, admittedly, unreasonably large bucket list.


Favorite Books

I was always interested in fantasy when I was a kid, but now as I've gotten older, I'm more interested in hearing peoples' stories and enjoy nonfiction, particularly autobiographies of interesting people or those who have overcome great hardship and impossible odds. The Man Who Broke Into Auschwitz is a brilliant example of this. Some of my favorites, in no particular order, are as follows:

  • The Heaven and Earth Grocery Store
  • The Man Who Broke Into Auschwitz
  • LOTR series/The Hobbit
  • A Woman In Berlin
  • In Order to Live
  • Atomic Habits
  • Becoming
